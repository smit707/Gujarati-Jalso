THE RESTAURANT


Our restaurant story is a wild ride to say the least. When we first started back in 1999 in a small one shutter shop, we couldn't have imagined being named the best Hospitality venue today. In a town long dominated by other food outlets, we have loved introducing our authentic taste services to our beloved customers. If you've never had your chance to grab a bite here with us, do join at the earliest. We are glad your taste buds have led you here to us. We are a traditional restaurant with a delightful ambience and soft mood lightening music to make your mood the best it has ever been. Whether ordering a meal to enjoy here at Gujarati Jalso, a large group order for your colleagues or a home delivery order for an morning in, you can trust that the quality will be consistent and we will exceed your expectations each time. Everything that goes in your mouth has been highly approved by our talented team of chefs and workers. We serve comfort foods for each and every occasion and season and with a lifetime of combinations of food items to put on your table, we're happy to be there for every savoury experience. Also,our central location near residential and commercial neighborhoods are a snap to locate and get to.

OUR VISION

We aim to connect the neighborhood to a dining experience in the same way ingredients are connected to food.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is simple: enhance and educate the palate with the freshest ingredients and flavors, while surprising and exciting each guest with personal care and service.

We believe the choices we make about what we eat, where it comes from and how it’s prepared have a direct and powerful impact on the health of individuals, communities and the environment.
